While typewriters may seem like ancient technology now, in the world of smart phones and laptops, there are many benefits that come from these machines.

Typewriters are distraction free. There are no emails or social media platforms to take your mind away from what you are writing. You can work freely on a novel, or a journal entry, or poem, or even a grocery list, without any distractions.

Typewriters are also used as ways of creating art pieces with their large variety of fonts, and refurbishing them themselves is an exciting and interesting experience.

But where does one begin? Where do you find a typewriter? And what do you do once you receive it?
